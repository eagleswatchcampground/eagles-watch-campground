Eagles Watch

Welcome to Eagles Watch Campground 🦅

This is a 12 site seasonal campground located on the Raystown Branch of the Juniata River.

Short drive to Lake Raystown. H&BT Rail Trail located near by.

We have approximately 300 feet of river frontage.

Sewage, water and electricity included in our seasonal rate.

Each site has 50 and 30 amp electricity.
